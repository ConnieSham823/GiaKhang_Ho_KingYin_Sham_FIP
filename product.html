Our Product

The Orbitz is not just a tribute to the classic but an exploration of innovation and cultural fusion. We hope you find familiar flavors in the new Orbitz, along with new surprises.

Mango Maple Tea

Combines the tropical sweetness of mango and lychee with the earthy richness of maple syrup, topped with delightful textures of chewy bubble tea pearls, tender coconut jelly, and herbal grass jelly.

Lychee Maple Tea

Blends the exotic, floral sweetness of lychee with the warm, earthy richness of maple syrup, enhanced by the delightful textures of chewy bubble tea pearls, tender coconut jelly, and herbal grass jelly.

Peach Maple Tea

Blends the juicy, sun-kissed flavor of ripe peaches with the warm, earthy richness of maple syrup, enhanced by the delightful textures of chewy bubble tea pearls, tender coconut jelly, and herbal grass jelly.
